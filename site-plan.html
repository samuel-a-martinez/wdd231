Cancun Cruise Trip Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: CancunCruiseTrip.org

Reasoning: This name is straightforward and clearly communicates the site's focus, which is a cruise trip in the Cancun area. The '.org' domain suffix suggests it may be a non-profit or informational site, which aligns with the project's purpose of providing trip details and resources.

Site Purpose

The Cancun Cruise Trip website will serve as a central hub for all participants of the trip. It will provide essential information such as trip itineraries, pricing details, and contact information for the trip's directors. Additionally, the site will feature recommendations for local tourist attractions and restaurants in the Cancun area to enhance the travel experience. A membership information form will be included for easy registration and communication.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: How much will it cost to participate in the cruise trip, and what is included in the price?
  • Scenario 2: Where can I find contact information for the trip's directors or other organizers?
  • Scenario 3: What are some of the recommended tourist spots and restaurants to visit in Cancun during our free time?

Color Schema

The color schema is inspired by the vibrant blue hues of the Caribbean Sea and the warm tones of the sun and beaches. This palette aims to evoke a feeling of relaxation and adventure.

  • Primary Color (#0077b6): Used for headers, navigation bars, and important call-to-action buttons. It represents the deep ocean and trust.
  • Secondary Color (#48cae4): Used for accent elements, such as links, highlights, and borders. It represents the lighter, clearer waters of the coast.
  • Accent 1 Color (#023e8a): Used for headings and subheadings. It provides a strong contrast and a sense of depth, like the open sea.
  • Accent 2 Color (#00b4d8): Used for hover effects and decorative elements, like section separators. Itâ€™s a dynamic color that adds visual interest.

Typography

The selected fonts are modern, clean, and highly readable. They are chosen to reflect a professional yet inviting tone.

  • Headings (h1, h2, h3): 'Poppins', a strong and geometric sans-serif font that makes headings stand out.
  • Body and Paragraphs: 'Montserrat', a clean and versatile sans-serif font that is highly legible for long-form text.
